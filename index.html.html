Common questions

Everything you
need to know

What is the investment?

Our wedding films are crafted for couples investing ₹3 Lakhs to ₹6 Lakhs+. The range depends on wedding duration, location, team size and deliverables. Every package is custom-built for your vision.

Do you travel for destination weddings?

Yes, absolutely. We have filmed weddings across Udaipur, Goa, Jaipur, Mumbai, Dubai, Bali and beyond. Travel and accommodation are arranged as part of your package discussion.

How far in advance should we book?

We recommend booking 3 to 6 months in advance — and for peak wedding season (October–March), even earlier. Our limited spots fill quickly once announced.

What deliverables do we receive?

A full-length wedding film, a short cinematic highlight film (typically 5–7 minutes), and a social reel. All delivered in high resolution, colour-graded to a cinematic standard.

How is the film delivered?

Your films are delivered via a private online gallery within 8–12 weeks of your wedding. You'll receive download links plus an option for a premium USB keepsake.

Can we meet before booking?

Yes — and we insist on it. Every couple goes through a personal connection call before any commitment. We want to ensure we're the perfect creative fit for your wedding day.
